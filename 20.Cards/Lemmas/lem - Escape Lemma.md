> [!lemma] Escape Lemma
> Suppose $M$ is a smooth manifold and $V\in\mathfrak{X}(M)$. If $\gamma: J\to M$ is a maximal integral curve of $V$ whose domain $J$ has a finite least upper bound $b$, then for any $t_0\in J$, $\gamma([t_0, b))$ is not contained in any compact subset of $M$.
