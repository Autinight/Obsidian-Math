
## Statement

> [!lemma] Lemma: 
> Supposet that $R$ is a root system in the real inner-product space $E$. Let $\alpha,\beta\in R$ with $\beta \neq \pm \alpha$, then 
>  $$ \left< \alpha,\beta \right>\left< \beta,\alpha \right>\in \left\{ 0,1,2,3 \right\}$$ 

> [!proof] Proof: 
> Thanks to [[Root System]] (R4), the product is an integer. 
> For any non-zero $v,w\in E$, then angle $\theta$ between $v$ and $w$ is such that $\left(v,w\right)^{2} =\left(v,v\right)\left(w,w\right)\cos^{2}\theta$, then

## Corollary

For each $\alpha,\beta \in R$, by relabling such that $\left( \beta,\beta \right) \ge \left(\alpha,\alpha\right)$, the probabilities are.

$$
\begin{array}{|c|c|c|c|}
\hline
\langle\alpha,\beta\rangle & \langle\beta,\alpha\rangle & \theta & \dfrac{(\beta,\beta)}{(\alpha,\alpha)} \\
\hline
0 & 0 & \pi/2 & \text{undetermined} \\
1 & 1 & \pi/3 & 1 \\
-1 & -1 & 2\pi/3 & 1 \\
1 & 2 & \pi/4 & 2 \\
-1 & -2 & 3\pi/4 & 2 \\
1 & 3 & \pi/6 & 3 \\
-1 & -3 & 5\pi/6 & 3 \\
\hline
\end{array}
$$
