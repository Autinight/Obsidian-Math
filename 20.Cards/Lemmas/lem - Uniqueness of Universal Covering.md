> [!lemma] Lemma
> Given two universal covering projections $p_i : \overline{X}_i \longrightarrow X, i = 1, 2$, there exists a homeomorphism $\psi : \overline{X}_1 \longrightarrow \overline{X}_2$ such that $p_2 \circ \psi = p_1$. A universal covering space, if it exists, is unique in this sense.
