

> [!theorem] Theorem: 
> Let $p:\overline{X}\to X$ be a covering projection and $\overline{x}\in\overline{X}$ be such that $p(\overline{x})=x$. Then the induced homomorphism $p_{\#}:\pi_{1}(\overline{X},\overline{x})\to\pi_{1}(X,x)$ is injective. 

> [!proofsketch] Sketch of Proof: 
> 对于同伦于零的道路, 利用纤维的离散性, 说明其提升同伦$\overline{H}$对端点的固定. 再利用常值道路提升的终点恰好落在离散空间$F$的性质, 说明了$\bar{H}$是到常值道路的同伦. 这就说明了单射性.
