
> [!lemma] Lemma
> Suppose $\omega_1, \omega_2$ are paths in $X$, with initial point $x$ and end-point $y$. Suppose that, $\omega_1 * \omega_2^{-1}$ lifts to a loop at $\overline{x}$, where $p(\overline{x}) = x$. Let $\overline{\omega}_1, \overline{\omega}_2$ be the lifts of $\omega_1, \omega_2$, respectively, at $\overline{x}$. Then $\overline{\omega}_1(1) = \overline{\omega}_2(1)$.

> [!proofsketch] Sketch of Proof: 
> 用已知提升限制出唯一的局部提升.

## Statement

> [!theorem] Theorem:
> Let $p : \overline{X} \to X$ be a covering projection of path connected spaces, $x \in X, \bar{x} \in \overline{X}$ be such that $p(\bar{x}) = x$ and let $F = p^{-1}(x)$. 
> * A loop $\omega$ at $x$ in $X$ can be lifted to a loop at $\bar{x} \in F$ iff the element $[\omega]$ belongs to $p_\#(\pi_1(\overline{X}, \bar{x}))$. 
> * There is a loop in $\overline{X}$ which is a lift of $\omega$ iff some conjugate of $[\omega]$ belongs to the subgroup $p_\#(\pi_1(\overline{X}, \bar{x}))$.

> [!proofsketch] Sketch of Proof: 
> * 基道路提升到回路, 当且仅当其同伦于某个回路的下降
> * 点 $\bar{x}$和能提升到回路的点相差一个基点变换.
