> [!theorem] Theorem: 
> Let $R$ be a root system and suppose that $B$ and $B'$ are two bases of $R$, as defined in [[Base for a Root System]]. Then there exists an element $g$ in the Weyl group $W(R)$ such that $B'=\{g(\alpha):\alpha\in B\}$.
