> [!theorem] Theorem:
> Let $X$ be connected, locally path connected space, admitting a simply connected covering space, $p:\overline{X}\to X$. Then for every subgroup $K$ of $\pi_1(X)$ there corresponds a covering projection, $q:Z\to X$ such that, $q_\#(\pi_1(Z))=K$.
