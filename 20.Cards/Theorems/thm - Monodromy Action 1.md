  
> [!theorem] Theorem:
> Let $p:\overline{X}\to X$ be a covering projection and $\overline{x}\in\overline{X}$ be such that $p(\overline{x})=x$. There is a surjection $\Theta:\pi_{1}(X,x)\to F=p^{-1}(x)$ which defines a bijection of right cosets of $K:=p_{\#}(\pi_{1}(\overline{X},\overline{x}))$ with $F$.

> [!remark] Remark: 
>  $$ \Theta \left([\omega ]\right)= \bar{\omega}\left(1\right) $$ 

> [!proofsketch] Sketch of Proof: 
> $\Theta$把路径对应到其提升的端点. 提升的端点相同就能拼出回路, 提升出回路就意味着$[\omega ][\tau ] ^{-1}$落在 $p_{\sharp }\left(\pi _1 \left( \overline{X}, \overline{x}\right)\right)$
