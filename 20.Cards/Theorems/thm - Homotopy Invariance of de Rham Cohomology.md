> [!proposition] Proposition: 
> Suppose $M$ and $N$ are smooth manifolds with or without boundary, and $F,G: M \to N$ are homotopic smooth maps. For every $p$, the induced cohomology maps $F^*,G^*: H_{\mathrm{dR}}^p(N) \to H_{\mathrm{dR}}^p(M)$ are equal.
