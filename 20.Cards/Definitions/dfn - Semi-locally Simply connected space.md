> [!definition] Definition:
> Let $X$ be a locally path connected and connected space. We say $X$ is **semi-locally simply connected** if each point $x$ of $X$ has a path connected open neighbourhood $U$ such that the inclusion induced homomorphism $\pi_1(U) \longrightarrow \pi_1(X)$ is trivial.
