## Definition

> [!definition] Definition: 
> Let $R$ be a root system. For $\alpha \in R$, $P_{\alpha }$ is the [[Reflection and Reflecting Hyperplane|Reflecting Hyperplane]]. We call $\gamma \in E$ **regular** if $\gamma \in E\setminus \bigcup _{\alpha \in R}P_{\alpha }$, **singular** otherwise.

## Properties

> [!proposition] Proposition: 
> If $\gamma \in E$ is regular, define
>  $$ R^{+ }\left(\gamma \right)= \left\{ \alpha \in R: \left(\gamma ,\alpha \right)> 0 \right\} $$
> Then 
>  $$ R = R^{+ }\left(\gamma \right)\cup  - R^{+ }\left(\gamma \right) $$
