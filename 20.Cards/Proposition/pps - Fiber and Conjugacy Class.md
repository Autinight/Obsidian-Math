

> [!proposition] Proposition:
> For various points $\bar{x} \in p^{-1}(x_0)$, the subgroups $p_{\#}(\pi_1(\bar{X},\bar{x}))$ of $\pi_1(X,x_0)$ are conjugate to each other.

> [!proofsketch] Sketch of Proof: 
> $\bar{x}_{1}$和 $\bar{x}_{2}$公用下降的落点 $x_0$, 故 $\pi_1\left(\bar{X},\bar{x}\right)$到 $\pi_1\left(\bar{X}, \bar{x}_{2}\right)$的基点变换下降为 $\pi_1\left(X,x_0\right)$的共轭作用.
