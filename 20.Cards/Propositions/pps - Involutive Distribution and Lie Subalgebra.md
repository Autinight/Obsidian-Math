> [!proposition] Proposition:
> Let $D\subseteq TM$ be a smooth distribution, and let $\Gamma(D)\subseteq\mathfrak{X}(M)$ denote the space of smooth global sections of $D$. Then $D$ is involutive if and only if $\Gamma(D)$ is a Lie subalgebra of $\mathfrak{X}(M)$.
